Benchmark Suite Contents

The benchmark suite is divided into three sections:
  • Section 1: Low level operations - measuring the performance of low level operations such as thread fork/join, barriers and synchronized methods/blocks.
  • Section 2: Kernels - short codes which carry out specific operations frequently used in Grande applications.
  • Section 3: Large scale applications - larger codes, representing complete Grande applications.
The contents of each of the sections in Version 2.0 of the benchmark suite are given below, together with a list of planned future additions. For further details of each of the benchmarks, please follow the links in the list below.

Section 1: Low Level Operations

Available in Version 1.0:

ForkJoin: forking and joining threads
Barrier: barrier synchronisation
Sync: synchronized blocks and methods

Section 2: Kernels

Available in Version 1.0:

Series: Fourier coefficient analysis
LUFact: LU Factorisation
SOR: Successive over-relaxation
Crypt: IDEA encryption
Sparse: Sparse Matrix multiplication

Section 3: Large Scale Applications

Available in Version 1.0:

MolDyn: Molecular Dynamics simulation
MonteCarlo: Monte Carlo simulation
RayTracer: 3D Ray Tracer
